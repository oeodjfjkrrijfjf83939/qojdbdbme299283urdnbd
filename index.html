How It Works

A streamlined, frictionless process built specifically to satisfy modern professionals, builders, and customer services.

01

Public Creation

Any client or user can securely structure their customized profile data in a simple public form using our responsive builder, without needing complex accounts or sign-ups.

02

Collect Custom Details

Link anything! Whether it is a global social link (LinkedIn, GitHub), an Indian specific platform (UPI, Paytm, Swiggy), custom certificates, forms, or maps location details.

03

Apply QR Theming

Admins can apply exquisite styles to each generated QR. Choose between 13+ unique shapes (glitch, fluid, hexagons) and seamless multi-color global gradients.

04

Scan & Share

Download the high-quality QR vector (SVG or PNG) and place it on your physical cards, leaflets, or tables. Your customer scans once, and accesses everything immediately!

About MultiLynk QR

A smart leap forward in digital identity and consolidated sharing.

MultiLynk QR represents a state-of-the-art step forward in digital business cards. It is designed to combine the flexibility of modern link managers with the security of role-based cloud databases. Instead of forcing clients to memorize countless social coordinates, MultiLynk packages their entire digital authority in a simple scannable visual token.

Whether you represent an enterprise client needing strict user limits, a small local merchant handling multiple payment options, or a professional presenting credentials, MultiLynk QR scales perfectly to fit your demands.
