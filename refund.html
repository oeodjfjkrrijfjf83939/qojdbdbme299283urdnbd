Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Cancellation Policy

There is a specific process to cancel any plan that has been opted for, whether it is a monthly plan, a yearly plan, or any other long-term plan.

Please contact our support team to initiate the cancellation process.

Refund Policy

Refunds are not automatic. Any refund request will be processed only after careful consideration and discussion between the owner and the business.

We aim to reach a fair mutual agreement in all cases.
